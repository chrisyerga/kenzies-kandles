✦ The Maker ✦

Hi, I'm Kenzie! 👋

I started making candles when I was 13, and I haven't been able to stop since! There's something so magical about blending scents and watching a blank jar transform into something beautiful.

Every Kenzie's Kandle is hand-poured in small batches using 100% soy wax and premium fragrance oils. I put so much love into each one — I hope they bring a little magic to your space. 💜
